LETS PLAY PIG!

    For those of you new to the game the rules are simple.

    The objective of the game is to get at or over 100 points before the other player does.

  • You get points by taking turns. In each turn, you repeatedly roll a die until either a 1 is rolled or the player passes and scores the sum of the rolls from that turn(i.e. the turn total).
  • At any time during a player's turn, the player is faced with two options: roll - If the player rolls a 1: the player scores nothing for the turn and it becomes the next player's turn. roll any other number: the number is added to the player's turn total and the player's turn continues. pass - The turn total is added to the player's total score and it becomes the next player's turn.
  • The first player to score 100 or more points wins the game!
